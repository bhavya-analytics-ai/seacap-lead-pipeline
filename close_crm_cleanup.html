What We're Doing
🏷️

Tag Every Lead

Add a custom field "Adam List" to every lead in Close. Script runs through all 228k, assigns List 1/2/3/4 based on their data and status.

🧹

Merge Duplicates

Find every duplicate by phone and email. Merge them into one clean record. All call history, notes, and activity preserved on the merged record.

🗂️

Replace Smart Views

Delete all 80+ source-based Smart Views. Create 4 clean ones — one per list. Each shows name, phone, email, status, last activity.

🤖

Automate Going Forward

Every new CSV dropped into the pipeline gets cleaned and pushed directly into the right list in Close. No manual work ever again.

🗑️

Remove Junk

Flag and remove records where the "name" is just an email address, leads with zero contact info, and fully blank records.

📊

Standardize Columns

Every Smart View shows the same clean columns — Business Name, Contact, Phone, Email, List, Status, Last Activity. Consistent across all 4 views.

🛡️

Nothing gets lost

  • Full backup of all 228k leads pulled locally before anything runs
  • No leads deleted — only tagged and organized
  • Duplicates reviewed before merging — nothing merged without approval
  • All call history, notes, emails, tasks preserved on every record
  • If a tag is wrong — remove it and the lead goes back to exactly how it was
